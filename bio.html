Izzy Savides is a multi-instrumentalist and producer from Detroit, who speaks a musical language of their own. Izzy’s music is a trip through the psyche, stopping at the darkest and most emotional centers of the brain. Their music fuses neo-soul, electronica, and grunge, with dense harmonies and heavy, downtempo riffs. Izzy draws inspiration from artists like Caroline Rose, Fiona Apple, and Radiohead. When hearing their songs, Izzy wants their listeners to feel absorbed into some deeper liminal space within themselves.
